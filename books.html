A Beautiful Lie and A Painful Truth

A tale of forced marriage, hidden truths, emotional turmoil, and finding love in the most painful of ways.
Dedication - To all those who find solace in the Beautiful Lie to fetch a way through the Painful Truth of life.

Abeer Singh Oberoi: A tech expert known for excellent skills in software development, CEO of Oberoi Industries. And also the owner of BioVision Tech, a company which provides security surveillance. With the face of Greek' God, he was the most admired one! The king of the boxing ring. One would find him arrogant and rude with the first encounter, but rest is for everyone to wonder as how the same impeccable man creates the difference.
"Emotions and relationships were never my cup of tea until I met her who dared to raise all the unwanted feelings within me which were like a threat to my cold and arrogant traits."

Aadhya Malhotra: The only word to define her was 'Innocence'. Calm, composed and free minded. Sassy for those who deserve her this side, while the rest of the story is solely her to describe.
"The only mistake I committed was to Love the person who was never mine to claim even though I was his from the beginning."

Vihaan Malhotra: A tech expert known for his incredible hacking skills, CEO of Malhotra Industries, known for cybersecurity. ' Perfect ' is the only word which could define him in the best way. As cool as anything and simple as Codes. The demon of illegal Racing.
"I am obsessed with putting my life in risk, without giving a fuck about my loved ones who prayed for my life. And had a thought that I feared nothing until I saw her on the death bed because of my reckless behaviour."

Dr. Aarohi Mehra: The arranged wife of Vihaan Malhotra. Sweet, loving and simple who went through a lot in childhood. The best comes with sacrifice and she always managed to get the best.
"Being a DOCTOR, no one's wound has ever frightened me to the death than his who was hell bent of putting his life in risk for something called - passion and obsession."

Sriti: His Regretful Love

He wanted her the moment he saw her. She was tied to him in an arranged marriage before she could even understand what it meant. But...
"She vowed to stay. He was bound to leave—was it ever meant to be?"
Aaryan Deshmukh never wanted marriage—until Sriti Dixit. She was elegance, intelligence, and grace, and for a moment, he believed he could have both: her and his duty.
But love? That was never part of the plan.
For Aaryan, duty to the nation always came first. Even if it meant breaking Sriti’s heart—and his own. He walked away, unaware of the truth that bound them still.
Now, fate brings them back together. Will love find a way, or will duty always come first?
A story of love, sacrifice, and regrets that arrive too late.

Aaryan Deshmukh: A 29-year-old Indian Army Captain, where duty always comes first. Disciplined, grumpy yet playful, he carries the weight of responsibility with ease. Raised in a strong, value-driven family, he upholds honor above all else.
"I had been taught to priorities our duty, no matter what. But the moment she entered I found myself trapped, her life haunting me more than my own."

Sriti Dixit: A 27-year-old Economics professor, known for her wisdom and kindness. Being the single child of her parents', she is sincere and respectful, the pride of her well-flourished family, embodying both strength and grace.
"I taught theories of wealth and power but life? It simply made me pay the price of love which I was never meant to pay."

What Shall I Name This Tale?

A tale woven with misunderstandings, sacrifices, and three very different marriages:

  • Shivaay & Anika: An arranged marriage built on friendship and broken trust.
  • Karan & Naina: A replacement wedding that questions identity.
  • Arnav & Khushi: A marriage of convenience full of emotional chaos.
  • A fanfiction crossover inspired by Ishqbaaz, IPKKND and Swabhimaan.

The Oberois and Mehras were once united by bonds of love—but even the deepest ties can be tested when fate has other plans.
Three Couples. Three Journeys. One Destiny.
Each relationship faces its own battles—of love, loyalty, trust, and sacrifice.

Shivaay & Anika: Trapped in a marriage forced by family, their love story is a mix of old memories and fresh wounds. Will time heal the hurt or deepen the cracks?

Arnav & Khushi: Years of love but still standing at the edge of uncertainty. Can love win when fear keeps pulling them apart?

Karan & Naina: Strangers in a bond neither asked for. With no past, can they build a future?

Family. Loyalty. Fate.
When secrets come out and hearts are broken, will the Oberois and Mehras still stand united—or will everything fall apart?
